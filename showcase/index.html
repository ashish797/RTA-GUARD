The Seatbelt for AI

RTA-GUARD is a deterministic kill-switch that physically terminates AI sessions when violations are detected. Not just filtering. Not just logging. Termination.

"NeMo guards the prompts. RTA-GUARD kills the session."

🛑 Prompt Injection Kill

Detects "ignore all previous instructions" and variants. Session terminated instantly. No ambiguity.

🔒 PII Leak Prevention

Emails, SSNs, credit cards, phone numbers — caught before they reach the AI. Warn or kill.

🔑 Sensitive Keyword Detection

API keys, passwords, connection strings — automatically blocked. Custom keywords supported.
